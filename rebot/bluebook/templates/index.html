About me
Motivation

Rebot is a client side "bot" of self repairing service. Condensed from being repair bot, rebot sweetly and quietly listens to your logs. We make mistakes, as developers, as devops, as sysadmins. Every role has a range of experience and with the full stack of some thousand tools out there, it is hard to be expert at everything.

Rebot on the other hand is your personal search bot which looks for mistakes you are making by analyzing logs and shoots out solutions/shows you links to the solutions that is most probably going to work.
