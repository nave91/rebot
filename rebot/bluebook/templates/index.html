Author Project
Motivation

Rebot is a client side "bot" of self repairing service. We make mistakes, as developers, as devops, as sysadmins. Every role has a range of experience and with the full stack of some thousand tools out there, it is hard to be expert at everything.

Rebot can help avoid overcome this problem by being your personal search bot. Condensed from being repair bot, rebot quietly listens to your logs for errors and shoots out solutions/shows you links to the most viable solutions that are going to work.
